# Brand Voice GTM Audit — Samotics Website

**Date:** 2 March 2026
**Scope:** 6 industry pages + site-wide ban list scan
**Reference:** Samotics Brand Voice GTM Extension v1.0

---

## Executive Summary

The industry pages are **structurally strong**. Proof points are concrete, installation language is credible, and the engineer test passes across most content. The main issues are voice emphasis ordering (energy efficiency positioned too prominently vs. the GTM priority stack), missing market-specific CTAs, unquantified claims in secondary copy, and ban list violations in blog/knowledge base content outside the industry pages.

**Pages ranked by compliance:**

| Page | Score | Critical Issues |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Energy Production (Oil & Gas) | 95/100 | Minor CTA wording only |
| Pulp & Paper | 92/100 | No violations; not in GTM doc yet |
| Chemicals | 88/100 | Hero lead has unquantified "cut energy waste"; minor label issues |
| Airports | 85/100 | Missing energy efficiency proof (voice pillar #3); vague detection windows |
| Water & Wastewater | 78/100 | Hero headline is defensive; voice emphasis order wrong; missing blockage CTA |
| Metals & Mining | 62/100 | Hero doesn't lead with downtime economics; missing vibration objection response; generic CTAs |

## Page-by-Page Findings

### 1. Water & Wastewater (Market A)

**What works:**
- £9.8M proof callout with named customers is excellent
- "Blockages," "submersible pumps," "pollution events" — language anchors present
- Installation proof ("20 minutes per asset") matches GTM mid-funnel block
- Results grid is fully quantified (£748k, £390k, 2,366 findings)

**What fails:**

| Issue | Current Text | Rule Broken | Fix |
|-------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Hero headline is defensive | "Protect water infrastructure without the guesswork" | GTM top-of-funnel pattern: should lead affirmatively with pollution prevention | → "Detect pump blockages before they cause pollution events." |
| Voice emphasis order | "Detect pump failures, blockages, and efficiency loss" (co-equal) | Energy efficiency should come LAST per GTM ordering | → Lead with blockage/pollution, then inspection reduction, then energy |
| Missing blockage CTA | No "Review a real blockage detection example" CTA | GTM requires this mid-funnel CTA pattern | → Add inline CTA linking to blockage case study |
| Unquantified energy claim | "energy optimisation" (Fans & Blowers section) | "If you can't quantify it, don't claim it" | → "7% efficiency gains as seen at Vitens" or remove |
| Vitens stat lacks context | "7% operational efficiency increase" | Proof needs mechanism context | → "7% efficiency increase — reducing pump energy consumption across 1,200 assets" |
| "Remote sites" anchor missing | Page never uses exact phrase "remote sites" | Language anchor underused | → Add to Scaling section |

### 2. Chemicals & Materials (Market B)

**What works:**
- Hero H1 ("Monitor equipment in ATEX zones — without entering them") directly addresses primary objection
- ATEX/cabinet language anchors used consistently
- Scan test passes — headings tell the ATEX → proof → scale story
- Primary objection pre-empted in multiple sections
- No ban list violations

**What fails:**

| Issue | Current Text | Rule Broken | Fix |
|-------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Hero lead unquantified | "cut energy waste" | Quantification rule | → "recover 5-35% in energy waste" (stat appears later — anchor it here) |
| Case study title vague | "Early warning on heated godet roll prevents $90k loss" | No quantified timeframe | → "Months-ahead detection on heated godet roll prevents $90k production loss" |
| Results label generic | "Energy savings from pump rightsizing" | Doesn't explain mechanism | → "Real-time cabinet data reveals pump oversizing — save €121k/yr" |
| "Predictive alerts" | "Receive predictive alerts weeks to months before failure" | Engineer test — they say "advance warning," not "predictive alerts" | → "Get advance fault warnings weeks to months before failure" |

### 3. Metals & Mining (Market C)

**What works:**
- "Every hour of downtime costs six figures" — strong quantified anchor
- 27 detected failures, 800% ROI, €650k saved — concrete proof
- Ban list clean
- Harsh environment / inaccessible motors narrative present

**What fails:**

| Issue | Current Text | Rule Broken | Fix |
|-------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Hero doesn't lead with economics | "Keep your mills running" (asset-first) | GTM: lead with downtime economics + planning | → "Every hour costs six figures. SAM4 detects failures weeks ahead." |
| No vibration objection response | Missing entirely | GTM requires complementarity + continuity narrative | → Add section: "ESA detects what vibration misses" |
| "Weeks to months" is vague | "weeks to months before failure" | Quantification rule | → Use specific: "5 months maximum advance warning" (stat already on page) |
| Generic CTAs | "See How It Works" | GTM: "Quantify avoided downtime for one critical line" | → Upgrade to market-specific CTAs |
| Missing language anchor | No "critical cooling water pumps" | GTM specifies this anchor | → Replace "process pumps" in relevant sections |
| "Comprehensive" adjacency | "Complements traditional monitoring" | Close to banned "comprehensive solution" | → Reframe as explicit complementarity |

- HVAC, baggage, plant rooms language anchors present
- Ban list clean

**What fails:**

| Issue | Current Text | Rule Broken | Fix |
|-------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Vague detection window | "weeks to months before failure" | Too wide a range for facilities engineer | → "5-14 days before failure — enough for one maintenance window" |
| "Immediate insights" unquantified | "Simple installation, immediate insights" | Quantification rule | → "Alerts within 24-48 hours of baseline learning" |
| No energy efficiency proof | Zero quantified energy claims on page | Energy efficiency is voice pillar #3 | → Add at least one HVAC energy stat |
| Case study lacks timeline | "detected belt degradation, bearing faults, and electrical issues" | No advance notice timeframe | → "3 fault types caught — 12-day median advance notice" |
