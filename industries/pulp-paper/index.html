The challenge

Harsh conditions and continuous production demand reliable monitoring

Paper mills are hot, dusty, and chemical-heavy environments that degrade equipment fast. Unplanned stops on a paper machine cost tens of thousands per hour. Traditional sensors struggle in these conditions.

Harsh operating environment

Heat, steam, chemicals, and dust make it difficult to place and maintain sensors on assets. SAM4 installs in the clean, dry motor control cabinet.

Continuous production pressure

Paper machines run 24/7 with planned shutdowns weeks apart. An unplanned stop means lost output and missed delivery windows. SAM4 provides advance warning to schedule repairs.

Energy-intensive processes

Pulp and paper mills are among the most energy-intensive industries. SAM4 identifies 5-35% energy waste in pumps, fans, and drives with real-time data.

How it works

Simple installation, immediate insights

SAM4 installs inside the motor control cabinet — no sensors on the asset, no production interruption, no confined-space entry.

  1. Install in the MCC Clip-on current and voltage sensors attach to existing wiring in the motor control cabinet. Takes 20 minutes per asset.
  2. Connect to SAM4 cloud The edge device samples at 20 kHz and sends data securely to the SAM4 platform for AI analysis.
  3. Get actionable alerts Receive predictive alerts weeks to months before failure, with fault type, severity, and recommended action.
