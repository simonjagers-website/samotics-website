The situation

What was at stake

At a chlor-alkali plant, circulation pumps keep highly concentrated salt water flowing — an essential part of the production process. If one pump fails unexpectedly, the entire production line shuts down. SAM4 was installed on several critical assets at the plant, including the belt-driven circulation pumps.

What SAM4 detected

The signal and the diagnosis

SAM4 flagged a sudden increase in spectral energy around the belt frequency of one circulation pump. The system provided the customer with a clear notification recommending that the maintenance team inspect the belt during the next planned stop. The notification included fault type (belt degradation), the indicator (belt frequency), and a specific action: inspect and replace the belt.

The outcome

What happened next

The customer inspected the pump during a planned maintenance window — no production losses, no emergency callout. They confirmed the belt needed replacement and swapped it out. The plant continued operating as planned. Early detection avoided potential damage to the asset and prevented a full production line shutdown.
