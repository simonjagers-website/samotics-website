Who we partner with

Four partner types. One technology platform.

Every partner type integrates SAM4 differently. The technology is the same — the commercial model and go-to-market approach adapt to your business.

System Integrators

Add continuous condition monitoring to your industrial automation projects. SAM4 integrates with the SCADA, PLC, and CMMS platforms you already deploy. Offer monitoring as part of the installation — not a separate procurement.

OEMs

Embed SAM4 ESA directly into your drives, motors, or control systems. The ABB ACS880 integration is the reference model — native firmware integration, no additional hardware. Your customers activate monitoring through your product.

Service Providers

Offer SAM4 as a managed monitoring service within your maintenance or reliability consulting practice. Remote diagnostics, fleet-level health dashboards, and automated alerting — delivered under your brand or co-branded with Samotics.

Distributors

Resell SAM4 hardware and subscriptions alongside your existing product catalogue. Samotics provides technical training, pre-sales support, and marketing collateral to enable your sales team.
