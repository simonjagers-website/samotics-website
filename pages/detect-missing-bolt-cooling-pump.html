Fault detection

Missing bolt caught early in 90 kW cooling pump

Asset Cooling pump (90 kW)
Fault detected Missing coupling bolt
Industry Industrial manufacturing
Detection to action 3 weeks

The situation

What was at stake

A 90 kW cooling pump was operating with a coupling that had lost one of its bolts. The missing bolt caused mild misalignment and unbalance — not yet severe enough to trigger alarms from conventional monitoring, but steadily worsening.

What SAM4 detected

The signal and the diagnosis

SAM4 flagged a slow rise in energy at the pump's rotational frequency. Samotics notified the customer and placed the pump on orange alert for close tracking. Weekly updates tracked the gradual energy increase. Three weeks after the first alert, energy spiked rapidly and the pump was moved to red alert. The customer inspected the running pump three days later — manual vibration measurements confirmed mild misalignment and unbalance. A stroboscope inspection revealed the missing coupling bolt.

The outcome

What happened next

The customer scheduled the pump for repair at the next planned maintenance stop. A single bolt replacement resolved the issue — no damage to the pump, no unplanned downtime. Early detection prevented what would have become a costly misalignment failure.

“It was great to get this insight so early, before it caused any damage and all we had to do was put in a new bolt.”
