Built for harsh conditions

Where vibration sensors struggle, ESA thrives

High temperature, heavy dust, constant vibration, and corrosive atmospheres destroy traditional sensors. SAM4 avoids these conditions entirely — because it never enters the production environment.

High temperature

Furnace areas, sinter plants, and casting halls exceed the operating range of most vibration sensors. SAM4 hardware sits in the climate-controlled MCC room.

Dust and particulates

Crusher dust, coal dust, and metal particulates coat and damage sensor surfaces. ESA uses the motor's electrical signature — no exposed sensors to contaminate.

Heavy vibration

Background vibration from crushers, mills, and heavy machinery creates noise that masks faults in vibration data. ESA reads a fundamentally different signal — unaffected by structural vibration.

Inaccessible locations

Underground conveyors, elevated fans, and enclosed process areas require scaffolding, permits, or production stops to access. SAM4 needs access to the MCC only — once.

What SAM4 monitors

Critical rotating assets across the process chain

From raw material handling to finished product, every motor-driven asset connected to an MCC can be monitored continuously.

Crushers & mills

Primary and secondary crushers, ball mills, and grinding circuits. SAM4 detects bearing wear, liner damage effects, and drive-train issues in the most punishing operating conditions.

Conveyors

Belt conveyors spanning kilometres of underground or surface transport. SAM4 monitors drive motors for bearing degradation, belt tracking issues, and overload conditions.

Fans & blowers

Ventilation fans, process fans, and dust extraction systems. Critical for mine safety and plant operations. SAM4 detects imbalance and bearing faults from the MCC.

Compressors

Air compressors and process gas compressors. SAM4 monitors mechanical health and efficiency continuously — catching valve faults and bearing degradation before performance drops.

Cooling water pumps

Process cooling circuits where pump failure means furnace shutdown. SAM4 provides continuous health visibility on assets that cannot afford to fail.
