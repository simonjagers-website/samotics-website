How it works

Connect once. We handle the rest.

SAM4 isn't self-serve software. From the moment hardware connects at the MCC, Samotics manages the entire pipeline — signal capture, analysis, expert validation, and alert delivery.

1

Connect

Hardware installed at the MCC captures the electrical signature of every connected AC motor. No sensors on the machine. No wiring to the asset.

2

Detect

AI models identify anomalies across the full drivetrain. A Samotics reliability engineer reviews every flagged anomaly — confirming fault type, assessing severity, and writing a recommended action.

3

Act

Validated alerts arrive in the SAM4 dashboard and push to your CMMS. Your team receives confirmed diagnostics with specific maintenance recommendations — designed to fit your existing reliability programme.
