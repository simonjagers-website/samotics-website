Fault detection

7 months advance warning on gearbox-motor misalignment in 160 kW pump

Asset Circulator pump with gearbox (160 kW)
Fault detected Gearbox-motor misalignment
Industry Industrial manufacturing
Advance warning 7 months

The situation

What was at stake

A 160 kW circulator pump was operating with a growing misalignment between its motor and gearbox. Over time, the misalignment wore out the coupling blocks connecting the two components. Without detection, the coupling would eventually fail — shutting down the pump and potentially damaging the gearbox.

What SAM4 detected

The signal and the diagnosis

SAM4 flagged a sudden increase at the rotational frequency of the gearbox's outgoing axle — indicative of misalignment. Samotics notified the customer and placed the pump on orange alert. Over the following seven months, Samotics sent weekly updates as the energy gradually continued to rise. When the customer's preventive maintenance cycle came around, they confirmed radial and axial misalignment between the gearbox and motor, plus badly degraded coupling blocks. No problems were found on other pumps during this check — which concurred with SAM4's assessment.

The outcome

What happened next

Seven months of advance notice gave the customer time to plan the repair. The alignment was corrected and coupling blocks replaced during a scheduled maintenance window. SAM4's accuracy on this pump — and its clean bill of health for others — provided evidence to shift from time-based to condition-based maintenance.

“Great evidence that SAM4 is reliable enough to help us move from time-based inspections and preventive parts replacement on every pump to condition-based maintenance only when a pump needs it.”
