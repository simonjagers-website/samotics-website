The key differentiator

Measured outcomes, not modelled estimates

Most energy savings claims are based on engineering models — assumptions about what should happen. SAM4 measures what actually happens. Continuously. Per asset. That distinction matters when an auditor asks for evidence.

Modelled
  • Based on nameplate data and assumptions
  • Snapshot in time (annual energy audit)
  • Cannot attribute savings to specific actions
  • Difficult to audit or verify independently
Measured (SAM4)
  • Based on continuous electrical signature data
  • Real-time baseline vs current consumption
  • Savings attributed to specific assets and actions
  • Auditable, timestamped, exportable evidence

Framework alignment

How SAM4 data maps to reporting standards

SAM4 energy data supports the most widely used ESG and sustainability reporting frameworks. The data is measured, not estimated — which is what auditors and regulators increasingly require.

GHG Protocol — Scope 2

SAM4 measures electricity consumption per motor asset. kWh savings translate directly to Scope 2 emissions reductions using your grid emission factor. Market-based and location-based methods both supported.

CSRD (EU)

The Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive requires auditable energy efficiency data. SAM4 provides asset-level consumption baselines, measured savings, and time-series evidence — the granularity CSRD double materiality assessments demand.

TCFD

Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures expects quantified transition risk and mitigation evidence. SAM4 data quantifies energy cost exposure across motor fleets and demonstrates measurable efficiency improvements.

CDP

CDP questionnaires require evidence of energy efficiency initiatives and their outcomes. SAM4 provides the measured kWh savings, CO₂ reductions, and investment payback data that CDP scoring rewards.

What SAM4 exports

The data your sustainability team needs

SAM4 doesn't just flag efficiency problems. It generates the specific data points that sustainability teams need for reporting, board presentations, and regulatory submissions.

kWh savings per asset

Baseline consumption vs current consumption, measured continuously. Every improvement is timestamped and linked to the maintenance action or optimisation that caused it.

CO₂ equivalent reductions

kWh savings automatically converted to CO₂e using configurable emission factors. Supports both location-based and market-based calculation methods.

Time-series efficiency data

Per-asset efficiency trends over months and years. Show auditors that savings are sustained, not one-time events. Identify assets with declining efficiency before they waste energy.

Export formats

CSV export for spreadsheet analysis. API access for integration with existing ESG reporting platforms and CMMS systems. Structured data, not PDF screenshots.
