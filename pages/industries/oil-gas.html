The challenge

Harsh environments make conventional monitoring impractical

ATEX zones, remote locations, and extreme conditions limit your monitoring options. Vibration sensors can't go where the assets are. ESPs sit kilometres underground. You need a different approach.

ATEX zone access constraints

Placing sensors on assets in explosive atmospheres is dangerous and often prohibited. SAM4 monitors from the motor control cabinet — safely outside hazardous zones.

Electric submersible pump failures

ESPs operate deep underground with no physical access. Failures mean lost production and expensive workovers. SAM4 monitors the full ESP drivetrain from surface.

Unplanned downtime costs

A single compressor failure can halt an entire processing train. SAM4 provides weeks-to-months advance warning on developing faults across the full drivetrain.

How it works

Simple installation, immediate insights

SAM4 installs inside the motor control cabinet — no sensors on the asset, no production interruption, no confined-space entry.

  1. Install in the MCC Clip-on current and voltage sensors attach to existing wiring in the motor control cabinet. Takes 20 minutes per asset.
  2. Connect to SAM4 cloud The edge device samples at 20 kHz and sends data securely to the SAM4 platform for AI analysis.
  3. Get actionable alerts Receive predictive alerts weeks to months before failure, with fault type, severity, and recommended action.
