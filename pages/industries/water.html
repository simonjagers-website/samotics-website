The challenge

Water assets fail silently — and expensively

Submerged borehole pumps, remote sewage stations, and ageing treatment works make manual inspection slow, dangerous, and expensive. Failures lead to pollution events, regulatory fines, and emergency costs.

Sewage pump blockages

Blockages cause overflows and pollution events. SAM4 detects blockage presence, location, and severity in real-time — before spills happen.

Submerged asset access

Borehole pumps sit 100-180 metres deep. Pulling them for inspection costs thousands. SAM4 monitors from the motor control cabinet — no crane, no diver.

Ageing infrastructure & pollution risk

Degrading rotors, worn seals, and failing bearings go unnoticed until they cause environmental incidents and six-figure fines.

How it works

Simple installation, immediate insights

SAM4 installs inside the motor control cabinet — no sensors on the asset, no production interruption, no confined-space entry.

  1. Install in the MCC Clip-on current and voltage sensors attach to existing wiring in the motor control cabinet. Takes 20 minutes per asset.
  2. Connect to SAM4 cloud The edge device samples at 20 kHz and sends data securely to the SAM4 platform for AI analysis.
  3. Get actionable alerts Receive predictive alerts weeks to months before failure, with fault type, severity, and recommended action.
