Bearing failure avoided in motor driving critical runout table roll

Asset Motor driving runout table roll
Fault detected Bearing degradation
Industry Steel manufacturing
Monitoring period 7 months

The situation

What was at stake

A motor driving a critical runout table roll in a steel mill showed early signs of bearing degradation. The customer had more than two dozen prior SAM4 detections on their fleet, so they trusted the system to track the fault progression and advise on timing.

What SAM4 detected

The signal and the diagnosis

SAM4 detected an increase in energy at the bearing cage (FTF) frequency and its harmonics. Samotics alerted the customer to developing but non-urgent damage and placed the asset under close observation. Over the next seven months, the customer received regular status updates. When the energy rise reached a level that warranted timely action, the customer replaced the motor and roll during a scheduled maintenance window. Energy levels returned to normal.

The outcome

What happened next

No unplanned downtime. The customer had the full picture of bearing degradation progression — from early detection through to recommended intervention timing. SAM4 has detected more than two dozen faults for this customer, building the trust needed to plan maintenance around its alerts.
