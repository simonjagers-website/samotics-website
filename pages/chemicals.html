The challenge

Access restrictions leave critical assets unmonitored

Chemical plants run continuous processes in ATEX zones. Installing vibration sensors means permits, shutdowns, and ATEX-certified hardware — so most motors go unmonitored. When they fail, the entire process line stops.

✗

ATEX zones restrict where sensors, cables, and junction boxes can be installed

✗

Production shutdowns required to mount sensors on rotating equipment

✗

Long procurement and certification timelines delay monitoring rollouts by months

✗

Continuous process environments mean every unplanned stop cascades across the plant

Fast deployment

Hours to install. No production disruption.

SAM4 connects at the Motor Control Centre — outside the process area. No ATEX-certified hardware in the field. No shutdown. No sensor mounting on the asset. Full fleet monitoring deployed in days, not months.

ATEX compatible by design

SAM4 hardware installs in the safe zone. No explosion-proof housings, no intrinsic safety barriers. The MCC room is outside the ATEX zone — so the monitoring system doesn't need ATEX certification.

No production shutdown

SAM4 installs on live circuits using non-invasive current and voltage sensors. Assets stay running throughout. No process interruption, no batch loss, no restart sequence.

Very low installation effort

30 minutes per asset at the MCC. No cable routing through the plant, no scaffolding, no confined space entry. Pfleiderer described the installation effort as "very low."

Scales without proportional effort

Adding assets means adding connections at the MCC — not mounting new sensors across the plant. Monitoring 50 or 500 assets follows the same installation pattern.

What SAM4 monitors

Process-critical motors across your chemical plant

Any motor-driven asset connected to an MCC can be monitored. SAM4 detects bearing degradation, mechanical imbalance, pump cavitation, and efficiency losses — from the electrical signature alone.

Process pumps

Transfer pumps, feed pumps, and circulation pumps handling corrosive and hazardous chemicals. SAM4 monitors health from the MCC — no sensors exposed to aggressive media.

Compressors

Process gas and air compressors in ATEX zones. SAM4 detects bearing wear, valve faults, and efficiency degradation without accessing the compressor skid or requiring shutdown.

Agitators & mixers

Reactor agitators and blending equipment running continuously. SAM4 detects seal degradation, bearing issues, and load changes that indicate process anomalies.

Fans & blowers

Ventilation, extraction, and process fans. Critical for safety and process control. SAM4 monitors imbalance, bearing health, and belt condition from the MCC.
