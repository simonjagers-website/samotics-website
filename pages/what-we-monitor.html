Platform capabilities

What SAM4 delivers — across every asset type

These capabilities apply regardless of equipment type or industry. Every connected asset gets continuous monitoring, AI-driven fault detection, expert validation, and actionable diagnostics.

Asset health scoring

Every asset displays a health score based on continuous electrical signature analysis. Green, amber, red — visible at a glance in the SAM4 dashboard. Drill into any asset for full diagnostic history and trend data.

Fault detection & classification

AI models identify anomalies and classify fault types: bearing degradation, misalignment, imbalance, cavitation, blockage, electrical faults, and process deviations. Over 90% of developing faults detected weeks to months before failure.

Energy optimisation

SAM4 identifies assets operating below their best efficiency point (BEP), oversized motors, and degradation-driven efficiency losses. Customers like Vitens have achieved 7.1% energy efficiency improvement using SAM4 data.

Predictive alerts

Alerts include fault type, severity, affected asset, recommended action, and estimated time to failure. Every alert is validated by a reliability engineer before it reaches your team. No false positives. No noise.

Fleet-level analytics

Aggregate views across sites, asset types, and fault categories. Track trends over time. Identify systemic issues. The asset map gives geographic visibility across your entire operation — from 20 assets to 10,000+.

Expert monitoring service

SAM4 includes a dedicated team of reliability engineers who monitor your fleet, validate every alert, diagnose root causes, and write specific maintenance recommendations. Plus a named customer success manager and quarterly business reviews.
