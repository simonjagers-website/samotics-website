Our story

How SAM4 started

2015

Started with vibration

Founded in Leiden to apply AI to industrial machine data. We built our own sensor system and started with vibration — the established standard for condition monitoring.

 

Discovered the gap

Vibration sensors need to be mounted on the machine. For submerged pumps, hazardous zones, and remote sites, that’s not possible. 30–40% of critical assets had no monitoring path.

 

Built ESA from scratch

We developed a system that reads the electrical signature at the motor control cabinet. After years of iteration and field validation, SAM4 launched — covering the assets vibration couldn’t reach.
