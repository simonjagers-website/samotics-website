Partners

				
		Trusted by global industry leaders

		
											
					

Partner

				
					

ABB

				
		ABB and Samotics have entered a strategic partnership to extend ABB’s asset health monitoring services to harsh and submersible environments. For the long-term the joint ambition is to reach the next level of condition monitoring by co-developing advanced digital service solutions focused on energy efficiency and operational resilience.

		
					See partnership page → (https://samotics.com/abb-partnership)				
																														
																														
					

Partner

				
					

Siemens

				
		Siemens Smart Infrastructure has integrated electrical signature analysis (ESA) from Samotics into NXpower Monitor, its industry-leading digital caretaker for electrical networks.

		
					See press release → (/news/siemens-enhances-nxpower-monitor-with-samotics-technology)				
					

Partner

		The Institute for Sustainable Process Technology together with Nouryon, Vopak, Samotics, TPA Adviseurs and the Universiteit Utrecht’s Copernicus Institute have received a grant to develop technology that reduces energy waste from industrial AC motors.

Go far together

				
					

Why partner with Samotics?

				
							
							- Raise reliability & lower lifecycle costs​

						
		SAM4's market-leading fault detection accuracy raises asset reliability, which creates a competitive edge in bids. It also helps prevent unplanned downtime, resulting in fewer warranty claims.

		
							
							- Complement your current offering

						
		You already offer condition monitoring? SAM4 expands both the faults and the assets you can monitor, so you can serve unique niches where traditional technology does not perform well or simply cannot be installed.

		
																														
																														
							
							- Easily integrate into your existing infrastructure​

						
		SAM4 is flexible, standalone technology that plugs smoothly into your existing platform via API. This provides your customers with the unique insights of our solution in the digital environment they already know.

		
							
							- Get operational insights

						
		SAM4 helps you unlock a wealth of performance and energy data that can be used with your existing products. This data also provides insight into how different customer segments use existing tech, and can support the engineering of future solutions.
