Electrical Signature Analysis

				
					

Use electrical data in the drive to see failures early

				
		Electrical Signature Analysis runs inside the ABB drive, turning current and voltage data into clear insight on developing electrical and mechanical faults across the entire powertrain.

		
					

No extra hardware install

				
					

Remove installation effort from monitoring

				
		Because the ABB drive already measures and sends the data, you avoid separate site work to mount sensors, route cables or add panels.

		
					

Full powertrain insight

				
					

Monitor pumps, fans, conveyors and mixers from one point

				
		By monitoring the power going into the motor, SAM4 ESA flags issues in the motor, transmission and driven load across common assets without touching the equipment itself.

		
					

Turn fault alerts into planned repairs with integrated ABB service

				
						
					

Turn drive alerts into planned maintenance

				
		Early warnings on developing faults give you time to bundle repairs into planned windows instead of reacting to sudden stoppages, lost output and possible fines.

		
																														
						
					

Spend less on equipment and callouts

Let local ABB experts act on the insight

				
		ESA findings feed into your ABB service contract so nearby engineers share the same view of risks and can help plan inspections, spares and repairs without extra admin on your side.
