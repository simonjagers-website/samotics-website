\#\# Claim Standard

A claim is valid only if it is:

1\. Technically defensible    
2\. Commercially measurable    
3\. Proven in a real deployment  

If one is missing → rewrite or remove.

\#\# Proof Hierarchy

Order of strength:

1\. Quantified customer outcome with name  
2\. Quantified outcome without name  
3\. Deployment scale  
4\. NPS / customer quote  
5\. Technical capability

Never the reverse.
