We are extremely proud to announce a successful kick-off with Aguas Andinas in Santiago de Chile.

Aguas Andinas provides access to drinking water and sanitation to the Metropolitan Region. Their focus is on sustainable social, environmental and economic development. Hence, they are the first Chilean company to adopt Electrical Signature Analysis (ESA) as a monitoring system for their pumps.

Together, we have just finalized the installation on their critical assets. The collaboration has been great and we look forward to the results of this project.

On to better operational efficiency in the water sector!
