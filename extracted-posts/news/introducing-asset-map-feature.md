We're excited to introduce the latest feature on the SAM4 platform—**Asset Map**! It's been our most requested feature by customers so far, and we're happy to launch it. Learn more about the feature and how you can enable it below.

With this new feature now you can:


 	- **Do a quick assessment:** See the real-time condition of all your assets spread throughout the region on an interactive map.

 	- **Prioritize maintenance:** Filter the map to only see assets with open red / yellow / orange alerts to pinpoint and prioritize those assets that need your attention the most.

 	- **Empower reliability decisions:** Look at the map with a "Historical view" to understand which assets often experience a lot of issues, and decide on long-term reliability solutions.



### Ready to use this new feature?


Whether you have 50 or 5,000 assets, we can enable this interactive map for you quickly and easily. We would only need to receive location coordinates of each site where we monitor your assets. Get in touch with your respective Customer Success Manager to activate it.

We can't wait for you to try it out and let us know what you think!
