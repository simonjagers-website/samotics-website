A proactive, condition-based maintenance (CBM) strategy holds great promise for reducing operational risks and O&M (operation & maintenance) costs. However, ramping up such an approach requires significant investment from asset owners, considerable managerial interventions and process changes to existing ways of working. Having a good sense of the value it will add is therefore one of the preconditions for a successful program—yet calculating the business case isn’t easy.

In this webinar, we'll be discussing the main value drivers of CBM and demonstrating practical methods to calculate its impact on your operations. Through detailed examples, including case studies from other water utilities, you'll learn how to quantify the benefits and make a compelling case for rolling out CBM within your organization.

Join us to discover the steps to validate the financial and operational worth of transitioning from a reactive to a proactive maintenance approach.
