Co-hosted with iAgua — Spain’s most-read water industry platform — this webinar tackles a pressing question for water and wastewater utilities: how do you keep pumps running reliably while energy prices climb and water scarcity grows?

You’ll see how electrical signature analysis (ESA) turns the data already flowing through your motor control cabinet into clear, real-time insight. With early warnings from ESA, utilities can cut unplanned pump outages, trim energy use and plan maintenance before faults escalate.

**What you'll learn:**


 	- The current challenges in maintaining water supply and controlling energy costs.

 	- How ESA works and why it's effective for condition monitoring at scale.

 	- Real-world case studies demonstrating improved pump performance, energy efficiency, and resilience.

 	- Practical steps for implementing ESA in your water utility to drive better outcomes.
