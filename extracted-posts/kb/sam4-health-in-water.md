# How SAM4 Helps Achieve Resilience and Energy Efficiency


Discover why water and wastewater companies choose Samotics as their trusted technology partner for actionable insights into asset health and operational efficiency.
## Why Water Utilities Partner with Samotics for SAM4


SAM4 provides the technology needed to achieve **net zero** and ensure **operational resilience**. Key features include:


 	- **24/7 remote insights** into asset health, energy consumption, and overall performance

 	- **Real-time alerts and advice** to prioritize critical assets.

 	- **Early warnings of developing failures** to optimize maintenance planning and prevent unplanned downtime

 	- **Long-term trend analysis and advice** for smarter energy and asset lifecycle decisions

## Where Water Companies Use the SAM4


Samotics is trusted by some of the world’s largest **clean water** and **wastewater companies** to optimize asset health, performance, and energy consumption. These companies rely on SAM4 Analytics to monitor a range of assets, including **inlet and storm screws**, **aeration rotors**, **blowers**, **borehole pumps**, **mixers**, and **centrifugal pumps** across their treatment plants, sewage systems, raw water, and distribution networks.

**With SAM4’s wide range of applications, here’s how it benefits water companies:**


 	- **Remote Monitoring Solutions for Submerged and Inaccessible Assets**SAM4 uses **Electrical Signature Analysis** to remotely monitor equipment remotely by analyzing electrical signals from the motor control cabinet. This allows SAM4 to track performance without physical inspections, making it ideal for monitoring hard-to-reach assets. Read more in our [ESA explainer](https://3777718.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/3777718/website-resources/esa-explainer.pdf?utm_source=samotics&utm_medium=pdf&utm_campaign=sam4-health-water-product-explainer&utm_content=&utm_term=).

 	- **Detecting Pump Clogging Remotely **We worked with our water industry customers to create a tool that remotely detects **pump clogging** as soon as it starts to develop — including identifying the severity, nature, and location of the blockage. SAM4 assesses the entire pump station to recommended the most effective actions. Discover our [clogging feature explainer](https://3777718.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/3777718/SAM4%20Health%20automated%20clogging%20feature%20explainer.pdf?utm_source=samotics&utm_medium=pdf&utm_campaign=&utm_content=&utm_term=).

 	- **Optimizing Centrifugal Pump Efficiency **In collaboration with customers across various industries, we developed a tool that remotely tracks **centrifugal pump performance** against its best efficiency point. This tool helps optimize operations, improve energy efficiency, and extend the lifespan of your assets. Get more details in our [pump curve explainer](https://3777718.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/3777718/sam4%20real-time%20pump%20curve%20product%20feature%20explainer.pdf?utm_source=samotics&utm_medium=pdf&utm_campaign=sam4-health-water-product-explainer&utm_content=&utm_term=).

 	- **Identify the Best Actions on the Path to Net Zero** SAM4 performs continuous energy audits across your entire fleet, pinpointing and prioritizing energy-saving opportunities based on their potential impact. This helps you take the most effective steps toward achieving net zero. Find out more in our [SAM4 Energy explainer](https://3777718.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hubfs/3777718/sam4_energy_water_brochure_final_version_nov_low_res.pdf?utm_source=samotics&utm_medium=pdf&utm_campaign=sam4-health-water-product-explainer&utm_content=&utm_term=).
