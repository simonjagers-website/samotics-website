## 💧 Meet Samotics at World Water-Tech 2026


Join Samotics at the World Water-Tech Innovation Summit in London on February 24-25, 2026. Our team will be on site to connect with water utility, infrastructure, and technology leaders who are working to strengthen reliability, performance, and sustainability across critical assets.

Whether you are exploring predictive maintenance or scaling AI-powered monitoring across your network, we would love to meet and exchange insights.
## 📍 Visit us at our booth


Visit us at booth 22 to see how Electrical Signature Analysis and AI are transforming condition monitoring in the water sector. Stop by to learn how Samotics helps utilities reduce downtime, cut energy waste, and extend asset life through continuous, non-invasive monitoring.
## 🎤 Presentation highlight


AI-powered asset monitoring: Delivering predictive intelligence for submerged pumps

📅 Tuesday February 24 | 🕑 11:50 am GMT | 📍 Main Stage, Auditorium 🎙️ Simon Jagers, Co-CEO and Founder of Samotics 🎙️ Simon Herrington, Engineering Performance Manager at Yorkshire Water

*Yorkshire Water’s submerged sewage pumps could not be monitored with traditional sensors, leaving the utility with no visibility, no early warning of failures, and a persistent pollution risk. By deploying Samotics’ SAM4 technology to monitor pump condition and performance, they eliminated pollution events, moved from reactive to predictive maintenance, and achieved significant energy savings. Join the talk  to learn how the solution was implemented, the operational changes it enabled, and the impact delivered across the network.*
## 🏭 About World Water-Tech Innovation Summit London


World Water-Tech is a global meeting place for utilities, regulators, technology providers, and investors working to address the most pressing challenges in water and wastewater management.

The event will feature expert speakers from across the global water industry; showcases of cutting edge solutions; insights into digital transformation and resilience strategies; and opportunities to network and build partnerships across the sector.
